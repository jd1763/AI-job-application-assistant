About the job
Microsoft’s Product Managers work with engineering and design teams to define, build, and launch features that impact millions of users.

Responsibilities:
- Define product vision and roadmaps.
- Analyze customer needs and market trends.
- Partner with engineers, designers, and marketers to deliver product features.
- Measure and report on product performance.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or Business.
- 2+ years of experience in product management or related field.
- Strong organizational and communication skills.
- Experience with Agile methodologies and tools like Jira.

Preferred:
- MBA or advanced degree.
- Experience working on cloud products.
- Demonstrated leadership in cross-functional teams.
