About the job
As a Data Scientist at IBM, you will work with large datasets, build predictive models, and develop machine learning solutions to support clients across industries.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement machine learning algorithms.
- Work with Python, R, and SQL for data cleaning and analysis.
- Collaborate with engineers and product teams to deploy models.
- Communicate results and insights to stakeholders.

Required qualifications:
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field.
- Experience with Python, R, and SQL.
- Strong knowledge of machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch).
- Excellent communication skills.
